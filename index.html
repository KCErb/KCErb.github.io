About Me

Why is the sky blue? Is e4 actually the best chess opening? Where do babies come from? Is the Oxford comma everything it's cracked up to be?

I am KC Erb and I know the answers to all of these questions (except the last one). I'm putting up this website for the same reason that indie band from across the street keeps stapling their poster to the light post in front of your house: because I can.

Feel free to click around get to know me a little better. I guess this website is kind of like a digital business card, but with more colors and less professionalism. I hope you enjoy your stay!
